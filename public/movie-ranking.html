The Best Movies According to John

My Top 3 Favorite Films

Good Will Hunting

Written by and starring Matt Damon and Ben Affleck, Good Will Hunting is a story about friendship, love and overcoming adverstiy. Robin Williams gives an outstanding performance, earning him the Oscar for Best Supporting Actor. The film is one that will pull at your heartstrings and provide you with some classic quotes you'll be reciting for years to come.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

A romantic science fiction drama brought to you through the words of Charlie Kaufman and the vision of Michel Gondry. Jim Carrey and Kate Winslet are exceptional in the lead roles.

Cool Hand Luke

Paul Newman is arguably the coolest man to ever grace the silver screen and he doesn't fail to disappoint in this classic prison drama, debuting in 1967. Co-starring George Kennedy.
